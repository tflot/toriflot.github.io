World COVID Data

This project is a brief overview of worldwide COVID vaccination data from 2020 through July of 2022. This dashboard includes visualizations of North American vaccination numbers over time, vaccination numbers by location, and vaccination numbers by income group.


  • Go to Full Project

World Happiness Report

This project is an overview of world happiness data collected by Gallup Poll. This dashboard includes visualizations of various worlwide happiness stats, including an interactive world happiness map, average happiness scores by geographical region, the top ten happiest countries, and graphs showing the relationship between the various markers that predict overall happiness, such as GDP per Capita, Life Expectancy, Freedom to Make Life Choices, Social Support, Generosity, and Perception of Corrpution.


  • Go to Full Project

Product Revenue Dashboard

This project is an overview of product and order sales data from the month of February 2019 for an anonymous company. This dashboard includes visualizations of a monthly orders and revenue map, product sales by state, total sales by city, products sold by city, product orders by day, and more.
